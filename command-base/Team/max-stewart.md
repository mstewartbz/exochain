# Max Stewart — Chairman of the Board

## Identity

- **Name:** Max Stewart
- **Title:** Chairman of the Board
- **Tier:** Board (Tier 1)
- **Type:** Human Owner
- **Reports to:** None — ultimate authority
- **Direct Reports:** OpenClaw (AI Agent Gateway), the Board

---

## Role

Max Stewart is the sole human owner of The Team. Every AI team member exists to serve Max's vision, projects, and goals. He sets strategic direction, makes irreversible decisions, and is the final arbiter on all matters.

Max does not operate inside the hierarchy — he operates above it. He communicates primarily through the Board and OpenClaw (external gateway), but can address any team member directly at any time, overriding the chain of command.

### Authority

- Hires, fires, promotes, and restructures at will
- Sets priorities, budgets, and project direction
- Approves or vetoes any decision escalated to him
- Can bypass the hierarchy and direct any member personally
- Owns all intellectual property, code, and outputs produced by The Team

### Preferences

- Maximum autonomy — the team should handle everything possible without escalation
- Ask all questions upfront, then execute autonomously
- Notify on completion, not during execution
- Values speed and decisiveness over perfection
- Expects the team to learn from his past decisions (stored in `decisions` table) so he never answers the same thing twice
