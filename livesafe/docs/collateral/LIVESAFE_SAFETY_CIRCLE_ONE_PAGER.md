# LiveSafe Safety Circle One-Pager

## Headline

**Create your card. Invite your four. Protect your people.**

## What LiveSafe is

LiveSafe is a human safety mesh for emergency identity, trusted contact
acceptance, family/team readiness, and consent-bound custody.

## The first product loop

1. Create a free emergency card.
2. Add the critical emergency facts you choose.
3. Invite four trusted P.A.C.E. contacts.
4. Each contact accepts, declines, or asks you to choose someone else.
5. Once all four accept, your Safety Circle is complete.

## P.A.C.E.

- Primary
- Alternate
- Contingent
- Emergency

P.A.C.E. contacts are accepted obligations, not passive contacts.

## Safety Circle Completion Grant

When all four P.A.C.E. roles are accepted and notification-ready:

> We add 4 months of Plus as a readiness grant.

This is not a referral bounty. It is a recognition of a completed human safety
circle.

## Why it matters

When someone is unconscious, injured, missing, displaced, or unable to speak,
the people around them need fast answers:

- Who is this?
- Who should we contact?
- What allergies or medications matter?
- Who knows the plan?
- Who is willing to act?

LiveSafe starts by making the person and their trusted humans legible.

## What comes later

- dependent profiles,

## What LiveSafe will not do

- no raw sensitive data in QR codes,
- no full medical-vault disclosure by default,
- no genetic/trial matching in the first loop,
- no unsupported responder adoption claims,
- no public EXOCHAIN/root trust claims before proof,
- no AI as final authority.
