# LiveSafe Safety Circle FAQ And Objections

## Is this a referral program?

No.

A referral program rewards traffic or signups. The Safety Circle Completion
Grant recognizes a completed readiness structure: four distinct P.A.C.E. roles
accepted by four trusted humans.

## Why offer 4 months of Plus?

Because the number mirrors the circle:

```text
4 roles
4 accepted obligations
4 months of Plus
```

The grant is a ritual of completion, not a bounty.

## Why not give 1 month per contact?

Because that turns the relationship into a stamp card. The circle is the
meaningful unit. Reward completion, not partial extraction.

## What if someone declines?

That is healthy. P.A.C.E. only works when the person is truly willing and able
to serve.

## Does accepting reveal medical records?

No. Accepting a P.A.C.E. role does not grant full medical-vault access. The
contact may only see information the subscriber explicitly shares or makes
available for emergency purposes.

## Should contacts be required to create an account?

They should create a free account to accept, manage, and revoke the role. The
account is part of consent and role management, not a marketing trap.

## Can one person fill multiple roles?

No. The circle should require distinct roles and distinct contact references.

## Can the subscriber name themselves?

No. P.A.C.E. must not self-grant.

## Can contacts revoke?

Yes. Revocation preserves the integrity of the social contract.

## Should the reward be public?

No. Keep completion private by default. Do not create leaderboards or public
comparison.

## What is the strongest language?

> Complete your Safety Circle and receive 4 months of Plus.

## What is the language to avoid?

> Get four friends to sign up and get free premium.
