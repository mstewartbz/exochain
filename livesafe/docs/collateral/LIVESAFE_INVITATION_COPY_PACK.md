# LiveSafe Invitation Copy Pack

## Subscriber Screen: Create Card Complete

```text
Your emergency card is ready.

Now make it stronger by inviting your four trusted P.A.C.E. contacts.
```

## Subscriber Screen: Invite Four

```text
Choose four trusted humans.

Primary: first route
Alternate: second route
Contingent: backup route
Emergency: final route

Each person can accept, decline, or ask you to choose someone else.
```

## Subscriber Screen: Grant Explanation

```text
When all four P.A.C.E. roles are accepted, your Safety Circle is complete.
We’ll add 4 months of Plus as a readiness grant.
```

## Invite Email: Full

```text
Subject: Bob named you as a P.A.C.E. emergency contact

Bob named you as a P.A.C.E. emergency contact.

This is not a marketing invite. Bob is asking whether you are willing to be one
of four trusted people who may be notified if Bob’s LiveSafe emergency card is
scanned or if Bob cannot speak for himself.

Your proposed role: Primary.

Accepting this role does not give you Bob’s full medical records. You may only
see information Bob explicitly shares with you or makes available for emergency
purposes.

You can accept, decline, or ask Bob to choose someone else. You can also revoke
later if your availability changes.

Create a free LiveSafe account to accept and manage this role. You can also
create your own emergency card.

When Bob’s full P.A.C.E. circle is complete, LiveSafe may grant Bob a readiness
credit. Your decision should be based only on whether you are willing to serve
in this role.
```
