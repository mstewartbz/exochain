# LiveSafe Create Card Invite Four Protect People

## Status

White-paper frame for the first public LiveSafe human-safety loop.

This frame is source-backed by
`docs/context/LIVESAFE_HUMAN_SAFETY_OPPORTUNITY_MODEL.md`,
`docs/context/LIVESAFE_ONBOARDING_AND_PACE_GROWTH_MODEL.md`,
`docs/context/LIVESAFE_PACE_READINESS_GRANT_MODEL.md`,
`src/human_safety_opportunity.rs`, and `tests/human_safety_opportunity.rs`.

## Core Frame

LiveSafe's first public expression is:

```text
Create your card. Invite your four. Protect your people.
```

The emergency card is the first artifact of care. P.A.C.E. contacts are the
first trust graph. The protected node is the first unit of readiness.

## Source-Bound Claims

- The first loop can be evaluated with synthetic references.
- The first loop does not require raw medical, genetic, contact, identity,
  location, trustee, vault, QR, or payment payloads.
- The first loop does not require full medical-jacket custody, clinical-trial
  matching, public EXOCHAIN trust claims, or responder-agency adoption.
- The P.A.C.E. Readiness Grant may recognize Safety Circle completion when it
  is framed as readiness recognition, not referral-bounty traffic.

## Metrics

The frame measures readiness before monetization:

- emergency-card creation,
- card save or print,
- core emergency-profile completion,
- P.A.C.E. invitation,
- P.A.C.E. acceptance,
- accepted-contact profile creation,
- dependent, family, or team readiness,
- review freshness,
- Safety Circle completion,
- Readiness Grant issuance.

`src/human_safety_opportunity.rs` keeps these calculations integer-only and
synthetic-data-only.

## Boundaries

This frame does not activate runtime reward issuance, billing, responder
disclosure, medical-jacket disclosure, genetic import, trial matching, agent
dispatch, EXOCHAIN writes, or public EXOCHAIN/root-backed claims.

Disablement path: remove this white paper, remove
`docs/context/LIVESAFE_HUMAN_SAFETY_OPPORTUNITY_MODEL.md`, remove
`src/human_safety_opportunity.rs`, remove `tests/human_safety_opportunity.rs`,
remove `pub mod human_safety_opportunity;` from `src/lib.rs`, and remove linked
test-plan and slice-map entries.
