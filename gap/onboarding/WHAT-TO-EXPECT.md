# What to Expect: The GAP Incubator

Welcome to the Governance Architecture & Policy (GAP) Incubator. This is an honest explainer of what you will experience over the course of this engagement. 

## What You Will Experience
This is not a traditional consulting engagement. You will not receive 100-page slide decks filled with theoretical frameworks. You will experience a rigorous, agent-driven engineering process. We operate in sprints, and we require your direct, decisive input at every critical juncture.

## What the Agents Will Do
Your Facilitation Team consists of specialized AI agents. They will:
- Interview you to extract your operational reality.
- Draft complex, machine-readable governance architectures (`Syntaxis` protocols and `NemoClaw` policies).
- Route those drafts through our internal 5-panel Decision Forum for constitutional validation.
- Present the refined, validated artifacts to you for approval.

## What You Approve
You are the ultimate authority. Our agents do the heavy lifting of formatting, checking invariants, and structuring the DAG, but **you own the policy**. 
You will cryptographically sign off on:
1. The Workflow Control Map.
2. The exact Authority delegations.
3. The Escalation triggers (who gets pinged when things go wrong).
4. The final Pilot Outcome Memo.

## What You Own at the End
When the engagement concludes, you will own a complete, production-grade governance architecture. The 6 core deliverables are not PDFs; they are executable, DAG-anchored configurations ready to be deployed into the MeshCore SaaS environment. You will own the definitive map of how your autonomous systems operate safely.

## What Happens After
Following the final approval of the artifacts, the Facilitation Team will spin down. Your configurations will transition from the incubator into your live operational environment (MeshCore), providing continuous, mathematically enforced governance over your organization.
