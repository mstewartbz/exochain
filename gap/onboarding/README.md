# CEO Onboarding Flow

The onboarding sequence is the CEO's first interaction with the GAP incubator. It is designed to be authoritative, frictionless, and immediately productive.

## The Journey

1. **Check-In**: The CEO logs into the environment. The `ceo-onboarding` Syntaxis protocol is triggered.
2. **Orientation**: The CEO is presented with the 'What to Expect' document and the Quickstart guide. This establishes the rules of engagement and the tone of the partnership.
3. **Team Activation**: The system dynamically spins up the Facilitation Team tailored to the CEO's profile.
4. **First Task**: The Engagement Lead introduces themselves and drops the ACRC Intake Form onto the dashboard.
5. **ACRC Execution**: The ACRC Facilitator runs the 8-indicator assessment based on the intake data.
6. **Council Brief**: The assessment is scored and briefly routed through the Decision Forum for a sanity check.
7. **The Pivot to GAP**: The Engagement Lead presents the ACRC findings to the CEO. Based on the results, the CEO formally authorizes the start of the 4-8 week GAP delivery engagement.
8. **Delivery**: The Artifact Builder begins generating the 6 core deliverables.
9. **Feeling in Charge**: Throughout the journey, the dashboard forces the CEO to cryptographically sign off on milestones. The CEO never wonders what the agents are doing; they dictate the pace.
