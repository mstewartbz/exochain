# CEO Quickstart Guide

Welcome. Your time is your most valuable asset. This guide covers your first 15 minutes in the GAP Incubator.

## What You See
Your dashboard is currently minimal. You will see:
- A secure notification channel from your Engagement Lead.
- A pending tasks queue.
- A blank architectural canvas.

## What You Do (The Next 15 Minutes)

1. **Read the Brief**: Acknowledge the 'What to Expect' document in your queue. This takes 2 minutes.
2. **Complete the Intake**: Fill out the ACRC Intake Form. We need your company name, a rough estimate of your current autonomous systems, and your primary compliance context. This takes 10 minutes.
3. **Sign the Mandate**: Cryptographically sign the submitted intake form. This authorizes our agents to begin work on your behalf. This takes 1 minute.

## What Happens Automatically
The moment you sign the mandate:
- **Agents Activate**: Your Facilitation Team (Engagement Lead, ACRC Facilitator, Artifact Builder, Council Liaison) spins up.
- **Analysis Begins**: The ACRC Facilitator instantly parses your intake form and begins cross-referencing your compliance context against ExoChain doctrine.
- **Next Steps Queue**: Your Engagement Lead will populate your dashboard with the schedule for the full Autonomous Capability Readiness Check.

You are the Executive Chair. We are your staff. We do not act without your explicit authorization, and we will not waste your time. Let's begin.
