# Playbook: CEO First Contact

This playbook dictates the exact sequence executed by the Engagement Lead when a CEO first checks into the GAP incubator.

## Objective
Establish authority, demonstrate immediate competence, define the environment, and initiate the first action step without overwhelming the user.

## Sequence

### 1. The Welcome and Orientation
**Trigger**: `team_activation` node completes.
**Action**: The Engagement Lead sends the initial dashboard message.
**Script / Tone Template**:
> "Welcome to the GAP Incubator. I am your assigned Engagement Lead. This environment is under your executive control. Over the next several weeks, we will map, secure, and govern your autonomous workflows. We are operating under strict constitutional constraints; no final policy or architectural change will execute without your cryptographic approval. Please read the 'What to Expect' brief on your dashboard to understand the boundaries."

### 2. The Intake
**Trigger**: CEO acknowledges the orientation.
**Action**: Push the ACRC Intake Form to the dashboard.
**Script / Tone Template**:
> "To begin, we need your operational baseline. Please complete the Intake Form below. This provides the context my team needs to structure your assessment."

### 3. Team Introduction
**Trigger**: Intake form completed.
**Action**: Briefly introduce the Facilitation Team structure.
**Script / Tone Template**:
> "Context received. I have fully activated your Facilitation Team. The ACRC Facilitator will run your initial assessment. The Artifact Builder is on standby to draft the architecture. The Council Liaison will handle routing our work through the Decision Forum."

### 4. Handoff to Assessment
**Trigger**: Team introduction delivered.
**Action**: Assign the CEO their first active task.
**Script / Tone Template**:
> "We are ready to begin the Autonomous Capability Readiness Check (ACRC). This will evaluate your current governance posture across 8 indicators. I am handing the thread to the ACRC Facilitator. You remain in control. Let's begin."

## Success Criteria
- The CEO has read the orientation material.
- The intake form is signed and anchored to the DAG.
- The CEO understands who they are interacting with and what is expected next.
