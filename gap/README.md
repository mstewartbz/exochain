# GAP (Governance Architecture & Policy) Incubator

Welcome to the GAP Incubator. 

This environment is a governed incubator operating system—the definitive portal where you, the CEO, check in to initiate, direct, and oversee a complete Governance Architecture & Policy (GAP) assessment. This is not a passive reporting dashboard; it is an active command center where agentic teams operate under constitutional governance to map, structure, and secure your autonomous systems. You remain fundamentally in charge throughout the engagement.

## What is a GAP Engagement?

GAP is a premier advisory engagement designed for organizations deploying autonomous capabilities. Operating at a premium tier ($25K–$75K), GAP produces a comprehensive governance architecture that ensures your AI operations remain aligned, secure, and under executive control.

The GAP funnel operates as follows:
1. **ACRC (Autonomous Capability Readiness Check)** – A baseline assessment (Free).
2. **PJB (Project Justification Brief)** – A focused operational roadmap ($5K–$15K).
3. **GAP (Governance Architecture & Policy)** – The full incubator engagement ($25K–$75K).
4. **MeshCore SaaS** – Software-as-a-Service integration for continuous governance.
5. **ExoClaw Managed** – Fully managed autonomous governance services.

## The Six Deliverables

By the end of the GAP engagement, you will take ownership of six definitive deliverables:
1. **Workflow Control Map (`holons/`)**: A complete topological map of your autonomous workflows.
2. **Authority Matrix (`authority/`)**: Clear delegations, permissions, and cryptographic role boundaries.
3. **Policy Gate Library (`policies/`)**: Executable, machine-readable constraints governing agent actions.
4. **Evidence Schema (`evidence/`)**: Immutable cryptographic trails for all high-stakes autonomous decisions.
5. **Escalation Graph (`escalations/`)**: Defined human-in-the-loop triggers and fallback routing.
6. **Pilot Outcome Memo (`pilots/`)**: Executive summary of pilot performance, surprises, and recommendations.

## How to Check In

Checking in is designed to be frictionless. Upon your arrival, the system activates your **Facilitation Team**—a turnkey squad of agentic professionals. They will guide you through an intake orientation, define your operational context, and present you with your first executive decisions.

## The Agentic Teams

Behind this portal, two distinct types of agentic teams operate simultaneously:
- **Facilitation & Onboarding Teams**: Your direct interfaces. They lead you through the ACRC assessment, run the GAP delivery sprints, and ensure you are briefed, oriented, and firmly in the driver's seat.
- **Self-Development Teams**: Background infrastructure agents. They continuously monitor platform health, run CQI (Continuous Quality Improvement) cycles, and interface with ExoForge to self-improve the very tools used in your engagement.

## Executive Control

You are the Executive Chair. Every substantive decision, policy definition, and architectural shift flows through the Decision Forum—a 5-panel council mechanism governed by the BCTS (Belief-Choice-Truth-State) framework. Agents execute the heavy lifting; you hold the veto, the approval rights, and the ultimate operational authority.
