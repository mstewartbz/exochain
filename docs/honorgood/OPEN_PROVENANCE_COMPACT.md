# Open Provenance Compact

Open provenance is the public-facing HonorGood posture:

We built. We use. We honor good.

EXOCHAIN records when upstream work materially informs downstream systems. Recognition may be attribution-only, offered terms, accepted terms, contributor-accepted terms, or a ratified agreement. The recorded legal effect controls settlement eligibility.

## Principles

- Attribution should persist while the upstream work is materially used.
- Contribution dignity does not require paywalls.
- Recognition does not imply a legal obligation unless terms and acceptance support it.
- Open-source license references must be preserved.
- Downstream systems must not claim EXOCHAIN protection by proximity.
- Humans approve new legal templates, disputed materiality, off-policy use, revocation, and ratification.
- Agents and holons may execute only inside delegated authority envelopes.

## Settlement Boundary

EXOCHAIN core records settlements. Adjacent surfaces may display or propose records only through a tested runtime path into core.
